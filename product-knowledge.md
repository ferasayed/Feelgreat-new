## BALANCE - Detailed Product Information (from official product profile)

### What is Balance?
Unicity Balance is a pre-meal drink with a patented fiber matrix that includes bioactive plant compounds, polysaccharides, and micronutrients. The fiber matrix is designed to help ease some of the impact that excess carbohydrates and cholesterol can have on the body. Balance is formulated to provide critical vitamins, minerals, and soluble fibers to help curb your appetite.

Tagline: "Balance: the smart solution to balanced nutrition"

### Key Features
- Biosphere Fiber: A proprietary blend of five soluble fibers (Guar Gum, Locust Bean Gum, Citrus Pectin, Oat Fiber, Beta-Glucans)
- Unicity 7x: A specialized blend of plant-derived polysaccharides (viscous soluble fibers that form a thick gel)
- Bios Cardio Matrix: A blend of plant extracts and phytosterols (Chrysanthemum Flower Extract, Policosanol/Sugar Cane Extract)
- Bios Vitamin Complex: A unique blend of vitamins and minerals (Vitamin C, A, E, B6, B12, Niacin, Zinc, Chromium, Biotin, Folic Acid, Thiamin, Riboflavin)
- Convenient, on-the-go single-serving sachets
- Subtle citrus flavor (Orange)
- Only 15 calories per serving, 5g total carbs, 3g dietary fiber, 3g soluble fiber, 0g added sugar

### Benefits
- Helps curb appetite between meals
- Aids in reducing cholesterol absorption into the bloodstream
- Helps reduce carbohydrate absorption in the body
- Supports normal, healthy blood glucose levels
- Supports healthy weight management
- Supports the 4-4-12 eating pattern and intermittent fasting

### Usage Instructions
- Take twice daily, 10-15 minutes before your largest meals
- Mix each packet with 8-10 oz (240-300 mL) of water
- Mix vigorously in a shaker cup
- Drink immediately

### How It Works
Balance includes viscous soluble fiber. When combined with water, it forms a thick gel as it moves through the digestive tract. The gel-like substance helps slow the emptying of your stomach, which results in prolonged satiety.

### FAQs
- Can take at other times of day or before snacks (many people consume fewer snacks between meals)
- Can still take if forgot before meal (better late than not at all)
- Must use recommended amount of water (choking risk without enough liquid)
- Suitable for adults wanting good health; pregnant/nursing should consult physician
- Contains natural ingredients, color may vary
- Compatible with food, contains chromium which may help maintain healthy blood sugar levels
- Can be taken with medications

### Ideal For
- Adults looking to increase their fiber intake
- Adults interested in maintaining healthy blood sugar and cholesterol levels
- Those looking to support their weight-management efforts
