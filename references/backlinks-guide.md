## Part 5: Link-Earning Content Assets

Create these high-value assets that naturally attract backlinks:

1. **Original Research Reports** - Publish findings from the Research Hub
2. **Infographics** - Visual guides on insulin resistance, gut health, etc.
3. **Free Tools** - Health calculators already on the site (link to /calculators)
4. **Expert Roundups** - Quote other experts, they'll link back
5. **Statistics Pages** - Compile health statistics with citations
