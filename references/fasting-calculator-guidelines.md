## Key schedule guidance extracted from the PDFs
- The program is built around **16:8 intermittent fasting**.
- The visual roadmap in the second guide shows a sample rhythm:
  - **7вАУ9 AM:** Unimate / calorie-free drink period
  - **12 PM:** Lunch
  - **4 PM:** Snack
  - **8 PM:** Dinner
- The guide also mentions a fasting window of **14вАУ16 hours**, so the calculator can position **16:8** as the main target while presenting it as a practical coaching rhythm rather than a medical prescription.

## Simple nutrition rules suitable for the calculator
### Focus on these foods
- **Lean proteins:** chicken breast, fish, shrimp, tuna, lean beef, eggs, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, tofu/soy options.
- **Vegetables:** especially leafy greens, broccoli, cabbage, cauliflower, cucumber, zucchini, mushrooms, peppers, celery, eggplant, asparagus, okra and similar non-starchy vegetables.
- **Healthy fats:** avocado, olives, olive oil, nuts, seeds, flax/chia, nut butters without added sugar.
- **Better carbs in moderate portions:** brown/red rice, quinoa, oats, barley, whole grains, legumes, berries, apples, citrus, kiwi and similar whole-food options.
- **Drinks:** water, tea, coffee without sugar; avoid adding milk/sugar during fasting hours.

### Eat in moderation
- Higher-starch carbs such as brown rice, oats, couscous, whole-grain bread/pasta.
- Higher-sugar fruits such as ripe banana, mango, pineapple, melon.
- Some fats/sauces like mayo or soy sauce should stay limited.

### Avoid or minimize
- Refined bread, biscuits, pastries, cakes, sweets, sugary cereals.
- Juice, soda, sweetened drinks, flavored sugary beverages.
- Fried meats / processed meats / heavily processed foods.
- Hydrogenated oils, margarine, microwave meals, ultra-processed snacks.
- Dried fruit in large amounts.

## Portion guidance from the PDFs
- **Protein:** about the size of the **palm**.
- **Carbs:** about the size of a **fist**.
- **Fats:** about the size of the **thumb** (except fluid oils/butter, still keep small).
- **Vegetables:** **one fist or more**.
- **Fruit:** **one fist or less**.

## Recommended calculator UX copy direction

- Add a disclaimer that this is **coaching guidance, not medical treatment or individual medical advice**.
