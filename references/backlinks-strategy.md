## 3. Citation Building Strategy

### Academic Citations
- **Google Scholar Profile:** Create and maintain a Google Scholar profile linking to research commentaries
- **ResearchGate:** Share research summaries and link to full articles on the site
- **ORCID:** Register for ORCID ID to establish research identity
- **PubMed Citations:** Reference PubMed studies in articles and get cited back through commentary

### Professional Citations
- **LinkedIn Articles:** Publish long-form content with links back to the Knowledge Hub
- **SlideShare:** Upload presentation decks from workshops with backlinks
- **Crunchbase:** Create a profile for the health education business
- **About.me:** Professional profile with authority links

### Local/Regional Citations
- **Google Business Profile:** Claim and optimize for "Health Educator" + location
- **Bing Places:** Mirror Google Business listing
- **Apple Maps:** Add business listing
- **Saudi Chamber of Commerce:** Business registration listing
- **Dubai Chamber:** If applicable for UAE operations

### Knowledge Graph Citations
- **Wikidata:** Create a Wikidata entry for Feras Alayed (requires notability)
- **Wikipedia:** Long-term goal — requires third-party reliable sources
- **Crunchbase:** Business/entrepreneur profile
- **IMDb:** If any video/documentary appearances
