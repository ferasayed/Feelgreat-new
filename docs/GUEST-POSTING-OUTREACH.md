## 📧 قوالب Outreach

### Template 1: Pitch Email

```
Subject: Guest Post Proposal: [Topic] - Arabic Perspective

Hi [Name],

I hope this email finds you well.

My name is [Your Name], and I'm a Therapeutic Nutrition Specialist
specializing in insulin resistance and metabolic health. I came across
[Website] and was impressed by your commitment to [relevant aspect].

I'm interested in contributing a guest post titled:
"[Proposed Title]"

This article would cover:
- [Brief point 1]
- [Brief point 2]
- [Brief point 3]

I've attached a brief outline for your review. This would be valuable
for your readers because [reason why it adds value].

I can provide:
- Original, unpublished content (1,500-2,000 words)
- Proper citations and references
- Author bio with link back to FeelGreat.us.com

Would you be open to this contribution?

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### Template 2: Follow-up Email

```
Subject: Re: Guest Post Proposal - [Topic]

Hi [Name],

I wanted to follow up on my previous email about contributing a guest
post on "[Topic]".

I've prepared a full draft and would be happy to share it with you
for review. I believe this content would be valuable for [Website] readers
given [specific reason].

Let me know if you'd like me to send the draft!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```
