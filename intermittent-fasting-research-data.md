# Intermittent Fasting Research Data for Studies

## Study 1: BMJ Network Meta-Analysis (2025)
- **Title**: Intermittent fasting strategies and their effects on body weight and other cardiometabolic risk factors
- **Journal**: The BMJ (2025 Jun 18;389:e082007)
- **DOI**: 10.1136/bmj-2024-082007
- **Authors**: Semnani-Azad Z, Khan TA, Chiavaroli L, et al.
- **Participants**: 99 RCTs, 6582 adults
- **Key Findings**:
  - All IF and CER strategies reduced body weight vs ad-libitum
  - ADF showed -1.29 kg vs CER (moderate certainty)
  - ADF lowered total cholesterol, triglycerides, non-HDL vs TRE
  - No differences for HbA1c and HDL between strategies
- **PMCID**: PMC12175170
- **URL**: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12175170/
